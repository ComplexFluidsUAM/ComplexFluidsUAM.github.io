Universally Adaptable Multiscale Molecular Dynamics (UAMMD)

Developed by Raul P. Pelaez as part of his PhD. studies, UAMMD is a generic multiscale modelling code running on NVIDIA Graphical processors. UAMMD can simulate the dynamics of millions of particles submerged in fluids on multiple spatio-temporal scales, including molecular dynamics, brownian dynamics, fluctuating hydrodynamics and more! UAMMD is open source and presented with a GPL license, you can check it out on github. Now UAMMD is used in several research groups all around the world and it is being actively developed by Raul and the rest of the complex fluids team.
